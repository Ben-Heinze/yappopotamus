#+TITLE: Prim's Algorithm
#+OPTIONS: toc:nil num:nil

*Prim's Algorithm.* Mark an arbitrary node as connected. Then repeatedly
pick the smallest-weight edge between a connected node and an
unconnected node; mark both endpoints as connected. Continue until every
vertex is connected.

The [[file:cut-property.org][MST Cut Property]] proves optimality directly: at every step, the
cut being considered separates the connected component from the rest of
the graph, and Prim always picks the cheapest crossing edge.
