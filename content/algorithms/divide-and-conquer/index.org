#+TITLE: Divide and Conquer
#+OPTIONS: toc:2 num:nil

Divide and conquer solves a problem by:

1. *Divide* — split the input into smaller sub-problems.
2. *Conquer* — solve each sub-problem recursively.
3. *Combine* — merge the sub-solutions into a solution to the whole.

The recursion terminates on trivially small sub-problems solved
directly.
