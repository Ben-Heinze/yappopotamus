#+TITLE: The Wonder Years @ Crystal Ballroom — Upsides & Suburbia Anniversary Tour
#+OPTIONS: toc:nil num:nil
#+DATE: 2022-02-05

The Wonder Years, Spanish Love Songs, Origami Angel, and Save Face at McMenamins Crystal Ballroom in Portland, OR on February 5, 2022. The Wonder Years played both /The Upsides/ (2010) and /Suburbia I've Given You All and Now I'm Nothing/ (2011) in full to celebrate their tenth and eleventh anniversaries — several songs hadn't been performed since 2015.
